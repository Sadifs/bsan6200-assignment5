PROJECTS

Paris Agreement Tracker (BSAN 6070 Final Project)
- Built a machine learning classifier predicting whether countries align with Paris Agreement climate targets using GHG emissions data from 1950–2024 across 39 major emitters
- Compared Logistic Regression (0.900 ROC-AUC), Gradient Boosting (0.885 ROC-AUC), and Random Forest; selected Random Forest after GridSearchCV optimization: 0.933 ROC-AUC, 84.6% accuracy
- Key finding: almost all on-track countries are lower-income nations with low per-capita emissions — validated against Climate Action Tracker ratings
- Built a live Streamlit dashboard with global mapping, country-level analysis, SHAP explainability, and interactive prediction tools
- Directly applies MEnvSc domain knowledge to a real climate policy question
- GitHub: github.com/Sadifs/BSAN6070-Final

Ask My Resume — RAG Chatbot (BSAN 6200 Final Project)
