PROJECTS

Paris Agreement Tracker — BSAN 6070 Final Project
Built a machine learning classifier predicting whether countries align with Paris Agreement climate targets using GHG emissions data from 1950–2024 across 39 major emitters. Engineered 8 predictive features from emissions time series including recent per-capita emissions, post-Paris trajectory changes, and year-on-year decline ratios. Compared Logistic Regression (0.900 ROC-AUC), Gradient Boosting (0.885 ROC-AUC), and Random Forest; selected Random Forest after GridSearchCV: 0.933 ROC-AUC, 84.6% accuracy. Key finding: nearly all on-track countries are lower-income nations with low per-capita emissions. Built a live Streamlit dashboard with global mapping, country-level analysis, SHAP explainability, and interactive prediction tools. Validated against Climate Action Tracker ratings. This project directly applies MEnvSc domain knowledge to a real climate policy question.
GitHub: github.com/Sadifs/BSAN6070-Final

Ask My Resume — RAG Chatbot — BSAN 6200 Final Project
